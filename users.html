Found a Bug?

Bugs in software happen. If you found some in KaratekHD, please report them into our Bugzilla. Please do not report bugs on forums or mailinglists - such reports will most likely be overlooked or forgotten by the developers quite soon. To make sure the bug gets noticed by developers and fixed as soon as possible, report it into Bugzilla.
